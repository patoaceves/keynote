// SECTION 03 · CRM — CASCADE

What is Cascade

Cascade is the CRM platform connecting Meta ad campaigns to dealer pipelines. Every lead generated flows automatically — assigned, sequenced, and tracked from first contact to close.

Lead Capture

Meta instant forms feed directly into dealer pipelines via real-time integration

Workflow Automation

Automated sequences assign leads, trigger follow-ups & route by salesperson

Sales Dashboard

Real-time pipeline visibility for dealer managers and sales teams

AI Bot

Conversational AI handles initial lead engagement and qualification

Digital Contracts

E-signature contracts deployed within CRM — piloted for MS Wisconsin

Link Monitoring

Constant validation that leads flow correctly from Meta into pipelines
