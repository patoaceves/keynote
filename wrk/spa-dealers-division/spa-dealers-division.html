Cascade is the CRM platform — built on the GoHighLevel platform — connecting Meta ad campaigns to dealer pipelines. Every lead generated flows automatically — assigned, sequenced, and tracked from first contact to close.

// Standard workflow types
01 · Ongoing
Ongoing Campaign
Enters via ongoing Meta campaigns. Static — updated only when client requests a change or coupon update.
02 · Event
One-Time Event
Built alongside the campaign. Turned off immediately after the event to prevent overlap with other clients.
03 · Factory
Factory Leads
Leads from Master Spas Corporate received via webhook and pushed into the CRM as directed.
04 · Nurture
Nurture Sequence
For leads that didn't convert. Keeps them warm with a long-term follow-up sequence. Not enabled for all dealers.
05 · Manual
Manual Entry
Pushed manually by a salesperson. Entry triggers a workflow based on the rep's selection at time of creation.
